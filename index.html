About me!
Hello, I'm Sunny Sæbø, an Oslo-based independent artist! I'm currently 22 years old and studying to be an art teacher. I've been drawing and creating my whole life, and I've studied art at three different institutions. My favorite medias are photography, traditional and digital drawing, and ceramics. I love capturing color and life in my artwork, with a focus on the body and human condition.
